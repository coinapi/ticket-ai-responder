Once you have successfully logged into your account, simply navigate to Subscriptions > Manage Subscriptions > Update Information (Under Billing Information). By accessing this section, you will be presented with the option to alter the account's name to your desired preference.It is important to note that this change will not only reflect the updated name within the Customer Portal but also extend its impact to future invoices generated for your subscription.
